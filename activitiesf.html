NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME (NSS)
The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an Indian government-sponsored flagship for public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. Popularly known as NSS, the scheme was launched in Gandhiji's Centenary year in 1969. Aimed at developing student's personality through community service, NSS is a voluntary association of young people in Colleges, Universities and at +2 level working for a campus-community (esp. Villages) linkage.

KERALA ENTREPRENEURIAL YOUTH SUMMIT
Kerala State Youth Welfare Board, an autonomous body incorporated under Government of Kerala invites you to become an integral part of the intellectually stimulating event as this will give you an opportunity to network with the icons of the Indian industry and global businesses. "KEY SUMMIT 2018 - KERALA ENTREPRENEURIAL YOUTH SUMMIT" by KSYWB is happening on 17th, 18th of January 2018 at Tagore Theatre, Trivandrum. The sessions have been designed in the spirit of entrepreneurship with a combination of shared best practices and participant interaction. 300 selected candidates will get the opportunity to participate this mega event and eminent speakers and iconic personalities from across the business spectrum will present their thoughts and share their experiences at these sessions. We aim to provide a forum for the youth of the state to network, share ideas and to provide an environment that will foster innovation and entrepreneurship that will help them to develop their own ideas. Whether in the context of starting their own business or working for a firm with an entrepreneurial culture. We endeavour to help the young talents to equip them for the challenges they face after graduation. Here we aim to show how the entrepreneurship world is becoming much bolder and more dynamic than ever before and how this development presents its unique set of challenges and opportunities. The event will accelerate the entrepreneurial spirit, creating jobs, adding value, and helping economies grow.

ADDITIONAL SKILL ACQUISITION PROGRAME - ASAP
Government of Kerala has initiated an ambitious Programme, the Additional Skill Acquisition Programme (ASAP) with the objective of tackling the issue of growing unemployment in the State.The Programmes aims at equipping selected school/college students with skills in Communication, IT and selected areas of industry and service sectors.
